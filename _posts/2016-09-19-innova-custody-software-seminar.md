Innova Limited hosted a highly anticipated seminar on September 19, 2013, to officially launch our cutting-edge custody software. The seminar served as an opportunity to showcase the innovative features and capabilities of the Innova Custody Software to key stakeholders in the financial industry.

The primary objective of the seminar was to provide an interactive platform for participants to gain in-depth knowledge about the Innova Custody Software and explore its functionalities firsthand.

We were thrilled to have key stakeholders from various financial institutions in attendance, including custodian banks, investment firms, and regulatory bodies.

During the seminar, our expert team delivered engaging presentations, live demonstrations, and interactive sessions to highlight the unique aspects and benefits of the Innova Custody Software. Participants had the opportunity to interact with the system, ask questions, and gain a comprehensive understanding of how the software can streamline and enhance custody operations.

We were delighted by the positive response and active participation from the attendees, who expressed great interest in the capabilities of the Innova Custody Software. The seminar facilitated valuable networking opportunities and meaningful discussions among industry professionals, further strengthening the collaborative spirit within the financial community.

At Innova, we believe in fostering strong relationships with our clients and stakeholders. The seminar served as a platform for us to engage with key industry players, understand their requirements, and gather feedback to continuously enhance our custody software.

We extend our gratitude to all the participants who attended the Innova Custody Software Seminar and made it a resounding success. Their presence and valuable insights have contributed to our ongoing efforts to deliver practical and functional solutions that address the unique challenges of the financial industry.
