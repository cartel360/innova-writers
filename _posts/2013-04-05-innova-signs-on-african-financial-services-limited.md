Innova Limited has been awarded the contract to implement for African Financial Services Limited (Zambia), a subsidiary of Sanlam Group, various software solutions including Fund Management and Unit Trust/CIS software.

As a trusted provider of innovative software solutions for the financial industry, Innova is honored to partner with African Financial Services Limited in Zambia.

This collaboration reinforces our commitment to delivering practical and functional software solutions that cater to the unique needs of financial institutions.

The software solutions we will be implementing for African Financial Services Limited are designed to enhance their operations in Fund Management and Unit Trust/CIS. Our expertise in developing comprehensive and customizable software solutions will enable them to streamline their processes, improve efficiency, and effectively manage their funds and unit trusts.

Being selected by African Financial Services Limited is a testament to Innova's track record of delivering high-quality software solutions and our ability to meet the specific requirements of our clients.

We are excited about this opportunity to work closely with African Financial Services Limited and contribute to their growth and success.

We extend our gratitude to African Financial Services Limited for placing their trust in Innova and selecting us as their software implementation partner. This partnership highlights our dedication to supporting financial institutions in achieving their business objectives through technology-driven solutions.

> Our expertise in developing comprehensive and customizable software solutions will enable them to streamline their processes, improve efficiency, and effectively manage their funds and unit trusts.

Innova is committed to providing ongoing support and collaboration to ensure a seamless implementation process and long-term success for African Financial Services Limited. We look forward to a fruitful partnership and the opportunity to make a positive impact on the financial landscape in Zambia.
