Innova Limited proudly contributed to and actively participated in Dev-Craft 2016, an esteemed event held in Nairobi, Kenya. With an audience comprising software engineers, project managers, product managers, quality assurance experts, data scientists, and UI/UX experts, Dev-Craft aimed to foster knowledge-sharing and elevate the standards of software development in Kenya.

As a company dedicated to technological innovation and excellence, Innova recognized the importance of collaborating with like-minded professionals in the software development space.

Our participation in Dev-Craft 2016 allowed us to engage with bright minds, exchange ideas, and contribute to the advancement of the software craft in Kenya.

During the event, our team members delivered insightful talks and workshops, leveraging their expertise and experience to share valuable insights and best practices. We aimed to inspire and empower fellow professionals, encouraging them to strive for excellence in their respective roles.

Dev-Craft 2016 provided an excellent platform for networking, learning, and staying up-to-date with the latest trends and advancements in software development. It allowed us to showcase our commitment to quality and innovation, while also learning from the expertise of others in the industry.

> Our participation in Dev-Craft 2016 allowed us to engage with bright minds, exchange ideas, and contribute to the advancement of the software craft in Kenya.

We are proud to have been a part of Dev-Craft 2016 and extend our gratitude to the organizers for creating an impactful event that promotes collaboration and knowledge-sharing within the software development community. Innova remains dedicated to advancing the software craft in Kenya and beyond, and we look forward to continued participation in such events that drive excellence in our industry.
